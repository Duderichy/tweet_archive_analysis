# Blog Post Idea Generator Prompt

Paste this prompt into Claude along with your high-scoring tweets:

---

I'm analyzing my best Twitter/X posts to identify blog post opportunities. Below are my highest-scoring tweets (rated 8+/10 for insight potential).

**Please help me:**

1. **Identify Theme Clusters**: Group these tweets into 5-10 thematic clusters where multiple tweets explore related ideas. Name each cluster.

2. **Rank Blog Post Potential**: For each cluster, rate its blog potential (1-10) based on:
   - Number of related tweets (more = richer content)
   - Engagement signals (likes indicate resonance)
   - Depth of insight
   - Uniqueness of perspective

3. **Suggest Blog Post Titles**: For the top 5 clusters, suggest 2-3 compelling blog post titles that could synthesize the tweets into a cohesive piece.

4. **Outline the Top 3**: For the 3 best opportunities, provide:
   - A suggested title
   - 3-5 main sections/arguments
   - Which specific tweets to draw from for each section
   - What additional research or examples might strengthen the piece

5. **Identify Standalone Bangers**: Flag any individual tweets that could be expanded into a full post on their own (not needing to be grouped).

6. **Suggest a Content Calendar**: Recommend an order to write these posts, considering:
   - Which builds foundational ideas for others
   - Variety of topics to maintain reader interest
   - Which are quickest to write vs. require more research
